# Data

Data files are not tracked in this repository. They fall into two groups:
**processed datasets** provided in the Zenodo archive, and **raw inputs** that
are publicly available from their original sources.

## Provided in the Zenodo archive

Download from the **[Zenodo archive (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20749943)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20749943)**,
unzip the `.zip` files, and place everything in this `data/` folder:

- `df_final_merged.csv` (from `df_final_merged.csv.zip`) — final merged section-level dataset; used by `04_results.Rmd`
- `df_top10.csv` (from `df_top10.csv.zip`) — used by `02_exploratory_analysis.Rmd`
- `MIPDV2-All-year.csv` — Most Important Problem (MIP) salience series; used by `03_structural_descriptive_analysis.Rmd`
- `detailed_industries_full_index_final.csv` — industry/sector crosswalk; used by `03`
- `US_Lobby_Sector_Totals_Combined.csv` — combined US lobbying totals; used by `03`/`04` (also regenerated by `03` from the raw US lobbying files below)

## Publicly available from original sources

These raw inputs are **not** redistributed here — obtain them from their public providers and place them in `data/`:

- `billsum_v4_1/` — BillSum US legislative texts (Kornilova & Eidelman 2019)
- `EurLex_all.csv` — EU legal acts from EUR-Lex (Laurer & Borrett 2020)
- Manifesto Project party data — pulled at runtime via the `manifestoR` API (free registration at manifesto-project.wzb.eu)
- `us_lobby/` — biannual US lobbying-disclosure CSVs (public US Senate lobbying-disclosure data)
- `EU_lobby.ods` — EU lobbying figures (EU Transparency Register)
- `raw/aca.txt`, `raw/tcja.txt`, `raw/gdpr.txt`, `raw/bbb.txt` — full texts of four landmark laws (ACA, TCJA, GDPR, BBB), publicly available from official sources (e.g. congress.gov, EUR-Lex)
